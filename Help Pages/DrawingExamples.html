Drawing examples

For drawing it is best to thing of the robot like it as a pen attatched to it that it can lift off of the screen or put down onto it. There are two ways that the robot can draw its path.
The default is the ideal path. It draws a dot at the start of each step, and drags a line between the dot and itself across the ground untill it reaches its destination where it drops its end of the line. It therefore draws the ideal pathe the robot could have taken between the points.

The actual path of the robot is not always strait. Not only is the robot not necessarily pointed strait at its target when it starts its step, but also there are other robots it has to avoid. The actual drawing simply draws dots at the robot position at regular intervals.
